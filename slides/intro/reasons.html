Encryption Approaches

Top-Down

Bottom-Up

  • There are basically two different approaches you can use when encrypting your data
  • You can either encrypt an entire database table or even the entire filesystem
  • This is a → Top-Down approch
  • Or you can encrypt only the sensitive parts of the data
  • This is a → Bottom-Up approach
  • With Top-Down - you have few keys, each used widely
  • With Bottom-Up - you have many keys, each used narrowly

Forgetting

GDPR

Backups

Throw away keys

  • So, what about forgetting?
  • Why do we need to forget secrets?
  • → GDPR can require us to forget
  • With a Top-Down approach you have the problem with backups; how do you forget a specific user?
  • With a Bottom-Up approach - you can instead → throw away that users key
