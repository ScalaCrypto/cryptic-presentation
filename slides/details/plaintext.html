case class PlainText(bytes: IArray[Byte], manifest: IArray[Byte])
  • PlainText holds the unencrypted data.
  • It also holds a Manifest, which will not be encrypted.
  • Put the secrets in the bytes, anything else in the manifest.
  • If an adversary knows something about what is encrypted, forced decryption becomes easier.
